Turing-PHD20

A showcase of Turing projects across student cohorts and research themes.

Posters and videos are now live!

The Turing is pleased to host on 24 September 2020 the first virtual research showcase led by its student community. This initiative aims to highlight the work being carried out by students who have been funded by the Institute through either its Doctoral or Enrichment scheme. This is an excellent opportunity for you to find out more about the on-going work at the Turing and forge new collaborations.

Register on Eventbrite to receive Zoom details and please support our students by attending at least 2 Q&A sessions.

For any queries please email us at student-posters@turing.ac.uk
